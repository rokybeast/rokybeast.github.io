About Me

Yo! I'm Roky — the guy who thought naming a programming language after his headset was a flex (spoiler: it totally is 💁‍♂️). I live in code — whether it's Rust, Python, or JavaScript — and if I'm not making a whole new language or breaking one I just made, I'm probably trying to design a UI that doesn’t fry my eyeballs.

My brain runs on logic, anime, and the occasional bug-fix at 3AM. Big fan of cryptography, custom tools, and turning "what if I built this?" into "wait I actually built this??". When I’m not coding, I’m watching One Piece (yes, I finished it all. Yes, I’m built different), going down YouTube rabbit holes on how random stuff works, or casually overengineering a to-do list.

I make stuff for fun, for learning, and sometimes just to flex on past me. If the internet’s a playground, then I’m the kid trying to build a rollercoaster with math and duct tape.
